About us...

MAS HUB CONGRESS is the annual flagship event of IEEE India Council which administers a platform student from institutions to show up together and share a common podium. This congress aims to kindle a spark in the minds of the attendees with its diverse and inclusive talks, expert sessions and panel discussions by notable speakers from all walks of life. The Congress not only focuses on highlighting the achievements and accolades of the nation but also primarily aims at bringing about the much needed welcome change in various fields. The Congress is an amalgamation of people all across India coming together and celebrating the beautiful blend of their cultures and vibrant ideas.
